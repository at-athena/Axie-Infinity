AXS (Axie Infinity Shards)

Introducing AXS, or Axie Infinity Shards, a vital component of the Axie Infinity ecosystem.

Utility

AXS is the governance token for Axie Infinity, allowing holders to vote on proposals and participate in the game's direction. Additionally, players can earn AXS through gameplay.

Staking

Holders can stake their AXS to earn additional AXS over time.

Burn Mechanism

A portion of AXS used within the ecosystem gets burned, reducing its total supply and potentially increasing its scarcity.

Axie Infinity: Uniting Blockchain and Content for a Boundless Future

We Are Axie Infinity

Axie Infinity is a decentralized game built on the Ethereum blockchain where players collect, breed, raise, and battle fantasy creatures called Axies. Integrating elements of traditional gaming and blockchain technology, Axie Infinity allows players to earn cryptocurrency through skilled gameplay and active participation. Its Play-to-Earn model has garnered significant attention in the crypto gaming space, making it one of the pioneers in blending the worlds of gaming and decentralized finance.

SLP (Smooth Love Potion):

Utility: SLP is primarily used within the game for breeding Axies. It represents the cost to breed and is consumed in the process.

Earning: Players earn SLP by winning battles in the game.

Burn Mechanism: SLP is burned when used for breeding, reducing its circulating supply.

Circulating Supply & Maximum Supply

Both AXS and SLP have their respective circulating supplies and maximum supplies, which can be checked on crypto market tracking platforms for real-time data.
